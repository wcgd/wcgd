Our Mission

Warren Clark Golfing Dreams is a registered charity, set up to deliver S.N.A.G. Golf (Starting New at Golf) and Tri Golf to disabled and disadvantaged individuals and groups.

Mainly through golf-related activities, we set out to make a difference to people’s lives, encouraging them to be socially active, and challenge themselves to take part in sport. Through our fun sessions, which are inclusive for all, we do team building activities, encourage people to socialise, have fun, reduce isolation, and spend time together.

Warren, the charity’s namesake, is a young man with Autism and learning disabilities. He is passionate about golf and represented Team GB at the Special Olympics World Games in Abu Dhabi 2019. Warren is a qualified S.N.A.G. Golf coach and loves to share his passion with others.

Introducing golf to a wide range of disadvantaged people is what Warren dreams to do, and what we do. In the summer of 2019, Warren Clark Golfing Dreams attended over 35 local events and introduced golf to over 6,000 people.

While spending quality time with family and friends, participants from all walks of life come together in our sessions—working as a team and learning to communicate. People with physical and learning disabilities and different faiths and cultures interact in our community sessions, where we teach golf, teamwork, and social tolerance.

Our Programs

S.N.A.G. Golf (Starting New at Golf)

S.N.A.G. Golf uses inflatable targets, oversized clubs, and simplified scoring to introduce the basics of golf to absolute beginners—perfect for people with physical or learning disabilities. Our S.N.A.G. sessions are mobile, so we can bring the inflatable field to community centers, schools, or local events.

Tri Golf

Tri Golf is an inclusive, coach-led activity for children and adults with disabilities. We adapt golf chips, putting, and game-based challenges to various skill levels. Tri Golf emphasizes teamwork, coordination, and confidence—perfect for schools, care homes, and disability groups.
